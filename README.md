# Home-loan-
# 1.Objective :-
The aim of this Power BI report is to analyze home loan applications and approvals to identify key factors affecting loan approval decisions, customer profiles, and bank performance.
This analysis helps financial institutions optimize loan policies, improve approval efficiency, and manage credit risk.

# 2.Key Insights

•	Overall Approval Rate: Around 68–75% of total applications were approved.

•	Credit History Impact: Applicants with a positive credit history had an approval rate above 80%.

•	Income Trends: Higher income applicants tend to receive larger loan amounts but approval is mostly credit-dependent.

•	Gender Analysis: Male applicants applied for more loans, but approval rates were similar across genders.

•	Property Area: Urban and semi-urban areas showed higher approval counts compared to rural areas.

•	Dependents Factor: Applicants with fewer dependents had higher success rates.

# 3.Business Recommendations

1.	Prioritize Credit-Ready Applicants: Maintain focus on applicants with verified credit history.
	
2.	Target Rural Growth: Design special loan plans to attract rural borrowers.
	
3.	Simplify Income Verification: Streamline approval for consistent income earners.

5.	Predictive Loan Scoring: Use ML-based models for faster and accurate risk evaluation.

#  4.Tools & Techniques

 This project is made using Power BI with the following tools:
 
•	Power Query – for cleaning and preparing data.

•	DAX formulas – to calculate total loan amount, approval rate, and default rate.

•	Charts and visuals – pie charts, bar charts, line graphs, and KPIs.

•	Dashboard filters – to view results by region, applicant type, or loan status.

# 5.Insights (Findings)

•	Most approved loans belong to people with steady jobs and regular income.

•	Self-employed people have more chances of rejection.

•	Urban areas show the highest number of approved loans.

•	Higher interest rates are mostly given to people with long-term loans.

•	People with short-term loans and fixed salaries repay on time.
